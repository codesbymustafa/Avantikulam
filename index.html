Discover Avantikulam

Avantikulam is a project aimed on providing free education for NTSE/Olympiad, JEE, and NEET aspirants through both online and offline classes.
We Provide :
1. Preparations will be done for classes 9 to 12 and JEE and NEET.
2. Students of all classes can register from any corner of the country through the website or app.
3. The team of experts will provide facilities of online classes, doubt solving sessions, recorded video lectures, practice test series, assignments, download portal, notes and books.
4. Regular mentoring and guidance will also be given.
